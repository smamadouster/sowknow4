# SOWKNOW User Guide

## Welcome to SOWKNOW

SOWKNOW is a Multi-Generational Legacy Knowledge System that transforms your scattered digital documents into a queryable wisdom vault using AI-powered search and knowledge graphs.

**Access**: [https://sowknow.gollamtech.com](https://sowknow.gollamtech.com)

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Create an Account

1. Visit [sowknow.gollamtech.com](https://sowknow.gollamtech.com)
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email, name, and password
4. Verify your email (if required)

### 2. Upload Your First Document

1. Click the "Upload" button in the top navigation
2. Select a file from your computer:
   - **Supported formats**: PDF, DOCX, TXT, images (JPG, PNG, TIFF)
   - **Max file size**: 100MB
3. Choose a bucket:
   - **Public**: Can be searched with Gemini Flash (cloud API)
   - **Confidential**: Only processed with local Ollama (private)
4. Click "Upload"

Your document will be processed and added to your knowledge base.

### 3. Search Your Knowledge

Use the search bar to find information across all your documents:

- **Natural language queries**: "What were the key decisions in Q3?"
- **Specific questions**: "Who is the project manager?"
- **Broad queries**: "Show me all contracts"

## Tips & Tricks

### Better Search Results

1. **Be specific**: "Q3 financial report" vs "report"
2. **Use names**: Mention people or organizations you're interested in
3. **Add context**: Include time periods or document types
4. **Use quotes**: For exact phrase matches

### Knowledge Graph Exploration

1. **Start with an entity**: Search for a person or company
2. **Click the node**: View detailed information
3. **Follow connections**: Explore related entities
4. **Check the timeline**: See events over time

### Collection Management

1. **Name your collections clearly**: "Q4 2024 Contracts"
2. **Use specific queries**: "contracts signed after October 2024"
3. **Pin important collections**: Quick access from dashboard
4. **Chat within collections**: More focused answers

## Understanding AI Responses

### Confidence Scores
- **High (80-100%)**: Well-supported by documents
- **Medium (50-79%)**: Some support but limited sources
- **Low (<50%)**: Limited or conflicting information

### Source Citations
Every AI answer includes:
- **Document references**: Which documents were used
- **Confidence level**: How certain the AI is
- **Caveats**: Any limitations or uncertainties

## Privacy & Security

### Confidential Documents
Documents marked as **Confidential**:
- Are processed with local Ollama only
- Never sent to cloud APIs
- Only visible to you (and admins)
- Stored encrypted

### Data Protection
- **Encryption**: All data encrypted at rest
- **Secure authentication**: JWT tokens with httpOnly cookies
- **No PII**: No personal information sent to cloud APIs
- **Audit trail**: All confidential access logged

## FAQ

**Q: How long does document processing take?**
A: Typically 1-5 minutes per document, depending on size and type.

**Q: Can I search in multiple languages?**
